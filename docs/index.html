Weekly Brief – 29 December 2025

Cybersecurity Events & Incidents

  • Critical MongoDB vulnerability CVE-2025-14847 “MongoBleed” is under active exploitation, affecting over 87,000 exposed instances worldwide and being linked to high-impact breaches such as Ubisoft Rainbow Six Siege internal compromise. Source: https://thehackernews.com/2025/12/mongodb-vulnerability-cve-2025-14847.html

  • Ransomware attack disrupted Romania’s national water authority operations across ~1,000 critical servers, indicating continued targeting of essential infrastructure. Source: https://www.techradar.com/pro/security/ransomware-attack-on-romanian-water-agency-hits-over-a-thousand-systems
